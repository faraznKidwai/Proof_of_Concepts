Challenge 3

I was reading this article by Tim Urban - Your Life in Weeks and realised just how little time we actually have.

In this challenge, a function is created that tells us how much time one has left to live assuming one reaches 90 years of age

It will take user's current age as the input and console.logs a message with time left in this format:


You have x days, y weeks, and z months left.


Where x, y and z are replaced with the actual calculated numbers.

The results are console logged.
